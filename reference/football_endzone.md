# Football Endzone

The endzones are the area beyond the goal line. Any offensive player who
is in legal possession of the ball while in the endzone, or who catches
the ball in the endzone, scores a touchdown for their team. This area
stretches from the back field boundary to the edge of the goal line
closest to the center of the field

## Usage

``` r
football_endzone(field_width = 0, endzone_length = 0)
```

## Arguments

- field_width:

  The width of the field

- endzone_length:

  The length of the endzone

## Value

A data frame of the bounding box of the endzone
