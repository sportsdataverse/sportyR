# Hockey Center Faceoff Spot

The center faceoff spot is the spot at which the game begins. Its center
should lie directly in the center of the ice surface. Its radius is
passed as a key in `rink_params`

## Usage

``` r
hockey_center_faceoff_spot(feature_radius = 0)
```

## Arguments

- feature_radius:

  The radius of the center faceoff spot

## Value

A data frame containing the bounding coordinates of the center faceoff
spot
